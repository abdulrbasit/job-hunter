# Research

Research a company before tailoring or interview prep. After completing, control returns to the calling workflow — caller must immediately continue the next step.

## Steps

1. Run 3–5 web searches. Use the job's role title to scope engineering and team queries:
   - `"<company>" product OR platform overview`
   - `"<company>" funding OR valuation OR revenue`
   - `"<company>" "<role title>" OR engineering culture OR tech stack`
   - `"<company>" recent news OR layoffs OR expansion`
   - `"<company>" "<role title>" expectations OR team structure OR day-to-day` (if above yield thin results)

2. Synthesize findings into `outputs/jobs/<slug>/company_research.md`:

```markdown
# <Company> Research

## Product & Business
<2-3 sentences>

## Tech Stack / Engineering
<what is known>

## Culture Signals
<glassdoor, news, blog posts>

## Recent News
<last 6 months>

## Application Angle
<one line on how this context informs the cover letter or story selection>
```

3. Return. Do not score or tailor — this skill only writes research notes.

## Rules

- Keep the file under 300 words.
- No speculation — cite or skip.
- control returns to the calling workflow after writing.
